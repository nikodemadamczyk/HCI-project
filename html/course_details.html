Human-Computer Interaction

CS-HCI-301
Enrollment Open
Credits: 6 ECTS
Semester: Winter 2024/25
Department: Computer Science

Course Description

This course introduces students to the fundamental principles and practices of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). Topics covered include user-centered design, usability evaluation methods, interaction design patterns, and contemporary HCI research areas.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand core HCI principles and theories
  • Apply user-centered design methods
  • Conduct usability evaluations
  • Design and prototype interactive systems
